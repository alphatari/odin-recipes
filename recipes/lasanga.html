Lasagna

Description

Another of my favorites, I first made this for Cruz when she was hungry. It was quickly devoures and has become an often requested meal.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Italian Sausage
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 28oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 oz. tomato paste
  • 1 Tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta
  • 1 cup shredded Italian cheese blend
  • 1 large egg
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Steps

    Step 1

  • Add the Italian sausage to a deep skillet and cook over medium heat until browned. While the sausage is cooking, dice the onion and then add it to the skillet with the sausage. Continue to stir and cook until the onion has softened.

    • Step 2

  • Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning to the skillet and stir to combine. Partially cover the skillet with a lid, turn the heat down to medium-low, and let the sauce simmer while you prepare the other ingredients. Stir the saue occasionally as it cooks.

    • Step 3

  • Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF. Add the ricotta cheese, Italian cheese blend, egg, and pepper to a bowl, then stir to combine. Set the cheese mixture aside.

    • Step 4

  • Fill a large pot with water and add 1/2 tbsp salt. Bring the pot of water to a boil. Once boiling, add the lasanga noodles and cook just until tender. Drain the noodles in a colander.

    • Step 5

  • Spread about 1 cup of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Lay three lasanga noodles on top of the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture evenly over the noodles. Repeat the layers two more times. Finish with the remaining sauce, then top with thh shredded mozzarella.

    • Step 6

  • Cover the lasanga with foil and bake for 40 minutes. After 40 minutes, remove the foil, turn the oven from bake to broil, and broil for about 5 minutes or just until the cheese gets a little brown on top. Watch the lasanga closely as it broils!

    • Step 7

  • Top the lasagna with chopped parsley if desired. Slice the lasanga into nine pieces and serve!
